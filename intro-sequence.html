Welcome to The Carpenter Movement

Where we help every follower of Jesus see their everyday work as worship to God.

The Problem

Many Christians struggle to see how their daily work connects to their faith. We feel like our "real ministry" happens outside of work, not in it.

But what if your work IS your ministry?

The Truth

God sees your work. Every email, every meeting, every project, every interaction with a coworker—it all matters to Him.

"Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men, knowing that from the Lord you will receive the inheritance as your reward. You are serving the Lord Christ."
— Colossians 3:23–24 (ESV)

Our Mission

The Carpenter Movement exists to help you see your work through God's eyes. We provide biblical theology, practical tools, and community to help you glorify God in everything you do.

Because your job matters to God, and it matters forever.

Ready to Begin?

Your work is worship. Your job is ministry. Your calling is right where you are.
