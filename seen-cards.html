Printing Instructions

  1. Click "Print Cards" above
  2. Print on heavy cardstock (67lb or higher)
  3. Print front, flip paper, print back (test alignment first)
  4. Cut along the guides to 3.5" × 2" size
  5. Optional: Round corners with a 1/8" corner rounder
  6. Leave in restaurant billfolds, books, or anywhere someone might need encouragement

Printing Instructions

Step 1: Print the FRONT page (next page) on cardstock

Step 2: Flip the paper and print the BACK page on the reverse side

Step 3: Cut along the dotted lines

Tip: Do a test print on regular paper first to verify alignment before using cardstock.
