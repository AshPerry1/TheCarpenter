Printable Work Cards

Pocket-sized reminders that your work is worship. Print these cards and carry them with you—in your wallet, on your desk, or in your toolbox.

Work as Worship

"Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men. — Colossians 3:23"

This shift belongs to Jesus. Work as if He's your manager—because He is.

God Sees Your Work

"God is not unjust so as to overlook your work and the love that you have shown for his name. — Hebrews 6:10"

Every email, every spreadsheet, every customer—He sees it all and it matters.

Not in Vain

"Be steadfast, immovable, always abounding in the work of the Lord, knowing that in the Lord your labor is not in vain. — 1 Corinthians 15:58"

Your faithful work has eternal significance. It will not be wasted.

Integrity Check

"The integrity of the upright guides them. — Proverbs 11:3"

Tell the truth in emails. Report hours honestly. Do what you say you'll do.

How to Use These Cards

  1. Click "Print All Cards" above or use your browser's print function (Cmd/Ctrl + P)
  2. Print on cardstock for durability (67lb or heavier recommended)
  3. Cut along the edges of each card
  4. Keep one in your wallet, desk, or toolbox as a daily reminder
  5. Share extras with coworkers who might be encouraged
