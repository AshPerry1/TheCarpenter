Stories from the Field

Real people from all walks of life discovering that their everyday work is worship. These are their stories.

"

I used to think my faith and my code were separate. Now I see every bug fix, every feature, every code review as an opportunity to serve my team and honor God.

Sarah Chen

Software Engineer

San Francisco, CA

"

Those long hauls used to feel meaningless. Now I pray for the families who will receive the goods I deliver. My route is a mission field.

Marcus Johnson

Truck Driver

Dallas, TX

"

Every lesson plan is an act of love for my students. I'm not just teaching math—I'm imaging God as I help His image-bearers flourish.

Emily Rodriguez

Elementary Teacher

Phoenix, AZ

"

I used to see numbers as cold and lifeless. Now I see my work helping businesses steward their resources wisely as a way to serve the common good and honor God with truth and precision.

David Park

Accountant

Atlanta, GA

"

Every patient I care for is made in God's image. When I tend to their wounds and ease their pain, I'm showing the compassion of Christ. My work is ministry.

Jennifer Williams

Nurse

Nashville, TN

"

Hospitality is holy work. Creating a place where people feel welcomed and fed—body and soul—is a picture of the kingdom. I'm serving God's image-bearers every shift.

Carlos Martinez

Restaurant Manager

Chicago, IL

"

Creativity reflects the Creator. Every design I make—whether it's a logo or a poster—is an opportunity to bring beauty and clarity into the world. That's a form of worship.

Rachel Kim

Graphic Designer

Portland, OR

"

Building homes for families to live in reminds me that Jesus was a carpenter. I'm providing shelter and safety—basic human needs. God sees that work, and it matters.

James Thompson

Construction Foreman

Denver, CO

"

Helping people regain mobility and strength is restoration work. It's a glimpse of the ultimate restoration God is doing in all creation. I get to participate in that.

Lisa Nguyen

Physical Therapist

Seattle, WA
