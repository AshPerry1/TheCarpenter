Daily Liturgy of Work

Monday at 9am: Six Micro-Practices for Your Workweek

1 Begin with Thanks

Practice: Start your workday by acknowledging that this shift, these tasks, and this opportunity belong to the Lord.

"This shift is Yours, Lord."

— Colossians 3:23–24

2 Name Your Neighbor

Practice: Pick one coworker or customer to quietly serve today. Look for a small way to love them well.

Who will I serve today?

— Matthew 5:16

3 Integrity Check

Practice: Commit to telling the truth in emails, estimates, and time tracking. Let your yes be yes.

Will I be honest in all things today?

— Westminster Larger Catechism Q141

4 Excellence Sprint

Practice: Choose one task to do beautifully—even if no one else notices. Do it as unto Christ.

What one thing will I do with excellence?

— Hebrews 6:10

5 Witness with Wisdom

Practice: Be ready to answer questions about your faith with gentleness and respect when the opportunity arises.

Am I ready to give an answer?

— 1 Peter 3:15

6 Close in Rest

Practice: At day's end, release outcomes to God. Trust that your labor in the Lord is not in vain.

"It is finished. My work is in Your hands."

— 1 Corinthians 15:58
